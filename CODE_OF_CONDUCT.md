# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free collaboration environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Give constructive technical feedback.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks or harassment.
- Discriminatory or hateful language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately using the contact listed in `SECURITY.md`.
